Concept: “Knowledge library”

Instead of support or connection visuals, this one feels like organized information.

A modern fintech visual showing a clean collection of floating information cards or help articles arranged neatly on the right side of the screen. The cards should vary slightly in size and position, creating the feeling of a searchable knowledge library. One card can be highlighted as selected while others sit softly in the background. The design should feel organized, helpful, and easy to explore with lots of spacing and no chat windows or dashboards.
